# New Year's Eve Concert

## Role
Major background event and likely pressure point for the case.

## Story Use
- The concert helps explain why the best officers are unavailable
- It provides a public setting with crowds, confusion, and high stakes
- It should remain a likely target, cover, or distraction tied to `The Mercenary`

## Design Notes
- Place it at a real Bern location once the exact venue is chosen
- Use it to create ticking-clock urgency throughout the session
- Link clues and incidents back to the concert whenever possible
