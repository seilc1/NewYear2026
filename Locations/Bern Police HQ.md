# Bern Police HQ

## Role
Opening location of the session.

## Scene Use
- The session begins in a meeting room inside the main police department
- The four player characters are gathered here by emergency pager message
- Lassard and Harris deliver the initial briefing here

## Tone
- Bureaucratic police setting mixed with parody incompetence
- A good place for awkward introductions, bad coffee, and administrative chaos

## Story Purpose
- Introduce the team
- Deliver the case hook
- Launch the investigation into Bern proper
