# The Mercenary

## Role
Primary villain introduced in the opening briefing.

## Function In Story
- Serves as the central threat driving the investigation
- Creates urgency before the New Year's Eve concert and citywide celebrations
- Gives the players a clear objective from the first scene onward

## Known Facts At Start
- A CCTV camera detected him entering Bern about one hour before the briefing
- The police do not yet know his actual objective
- Harris assumes that something bad will happen soon

## Use In Play
- Keep his plan hidden at first and reveal it through clues, witnesses, and failed attempts to stop him
- Let his actions trigger comedic chaos across real Bern locations
- Build him as dangerous enough to matter, but still compatible with parody and over-the-top scenes
