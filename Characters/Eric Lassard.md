# Eric Lassard

## Role
Senior police leader who opens the session briefing.

## Function In Story
- Greets the player characters in an overly warm and wildly unhelpful way
- Signals the `Police Academy` parody tone immediately
- Dumps responsibility onto Harris and exits before any useful detail is provided

## Performance Notes
- Cheerful, grandfatherly, and completely disconnected from operational reality
- Speaks as if everything is delightful, even in the middle of a crisis
- Encourages the team in a way that sounds supportive but solves nothing

## Opening Scene Use
- Enter the room after the four player characters are already seated
- Deliver the friendly but chaotic introduction
- Leave the room and reveal Harris waiting behind him
