# Mirjam Kessler

## Role
Private security employee in the federal quarter.

## Function In Story
- Act 2 witness for the growing false-emergency narrative
- Bridges the gap between fake paperwork and the later bank-access reveal

## What She Knows
- She heard informal talk about emergency perimeter changes before any official confirmation existed
- The instructions sounded plausible but came through a chain nobody could clearly identify afterward
- She became suspicious because everyone assumed someone else had authorized it

## Performance Notes
- Careful, tense, and frustrated that security communication was so sloppy
- Wants to help, but worries she will be blamed for not escalating earlier

- Primary witness in `Story Elements/Swiss National Bank Robbery Scenario.md` Act 2
