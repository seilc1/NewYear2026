# Moses Hightower

## Role
Head of Forensic Services and section head for `Samira Aebi`.

## Function In Story
- Represents calm competence and technical seriousness inside a department that often runs on noise and panic
- Gives the forensic rookie a superior who values patience, evidence, and discipline
- Reinforces that physical traces and technical details can break the case open before the final showdown

## Relationship To Rookie
- Trusts Samira's eye for evidence, but knows she has limited field experience
- Wants her to learn how to stay accurate when the scene is messy, public, and rushed
- Allowed her onto the `B team` because this case will go badly if nobody on it understands what the physical evidence means

## Performance Notes
- Calm, imposing, and difficult to rattle
- Speaks clearly and does not waste words
- Feels protective toward younger officers without becoming soft or sentimental

## Opening Scene Or Background Use
- Can be referenced as the section head who insisted that somebody technically trained had to be attached to the team
- His name helps ground Samira's role as a serious investigator rather than a comic lab stereotype
