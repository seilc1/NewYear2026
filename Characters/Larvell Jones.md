# Larvell Jones

## Role
Head of Records and Evidence Administration and section head for `Timon Keller`.

## Function In Story
- Represents the side of the department that knows where the paperwork is supposed to lead, even when nobody else reads it properly
- Gives the records rookie a superior who understands that administrative mistakes can expose the whole operation
- Reinforces that forged documents and bad internal handling are central to the investigation

## Relationship To Rookie
- Likes that Timon notices details without needing constant supervision
- Thinks Timon has good instincts, but needs more confidence when challenging louder officers
- Sent him along because somebody on this team needs to understand what the paperwork actually says

## Performance Notes
- Friendly, perceptive, and better at reading a room than people expect
- Carries himself with relaxed confidence rather than obvious rank-heavy authority
- Treats support work as real police work and expects others to stop underestimating it

## Opening Scene Or Background Use
- Can be referenced as the section head who warned that Harris would ignore the files until they became a disaster
- His presence in the background helps frame Timon as useful, not accidental
