# Debbie Callahan

## Role
Head of Traffic Control and section head for `Nina Frei`.

## Function In Story
- Represents discipline, command presence, and practical urban control work inside the department
- Gives the traffic rookie a superior who understands how much a city can be manipulated through routes, barriers, and timing
- Reinforces that logistics and movement are vital to solving the case, not just background procedure

## Relationship To Rookie
- Respects Nina's eye for routes and timing, but expects her to speak with more confidence
- Thinks Nina knows her material, but still hesitates too much when senior people are wrong
- Sent her to the `B team` because this case may reward somebody who notices the hidden consequences of bad route orders

## Performance Notes
- Controlled, direct, and difficult to intimidate
- Expects competence and clear thinking from everybody around her
- Comes across as tough, but she is more supportive than she first appears

## Opening Scene Or Background Use
- Can be mentioned as the officer who immediately understood that fake route orders could be more dangerous than obvious violence
- Her name on Nina's background helps establish that traffic control is a serious section, not a joke assignment
