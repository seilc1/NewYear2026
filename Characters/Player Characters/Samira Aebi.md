# Samira Aebi

## Role
Player-character template for a forensic services rookie.

## Section
Forensic Services.

## Section Head
`Moses Hightower` in `../Moses Hightower.md`

## Function In Story
- Gives the team somebody who can interpret traces, tool use, damaged equipment, and technical preparation
- Helps the group understand how a sabotage, break-in, or staged incident was meant to work
- Works especially well in scenes involving tools, tampering, technical gear, or a crime scene that needs more than guesswork

## What They Are Good At
- Reading physical traces and signs of tampering
- Distinguishing useful technical equipment from bluff, junk, or decoys
- Explaining what kind of preparation a criminal needed before entering a scene
- Staying focused when the rest of the team gets distracted by noise or panic

## Why They Ended Up On The B Team
- Still too inexperienced to be sent out alone on major forensic work
- Technically trained, but not yet comfortable with the chaos of live police operations
- Was reassigned because senior forensic staff were already tied up on more urgent official cases

## How To Play Them
- Play Samira as analytical, composed, and careful with conclusions
- She does not need to be socially awkward, but she should prefer evidence over speculation
- She can be quietly direct when the team is making the wrong assumption
- She wants to prove that proper technical work can stop a crime before the final disaster starts

## Opening Scene Use
- Samira is the one most likely to ask what actual evidence the department has instead of what Harris assumes
- Harris can treat her as the rookie technician who is about to discover that fieldwork is louder than the lab
