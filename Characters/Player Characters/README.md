# Player Characters

These files provide the four player-character templates for the Bern police department's improvised `B team`.

- The player characters are rookies from different sections
- Each starts with a full name, section, and section head
- Each template leaves room for the player to define personal history, exact appearance, and deeper personality details
- The core idea is that every rookie brings one practical competence that can help solve `The Mercenary`'s case

Current player-character templates:

- `Luca Baumann.md` - patrol rookie under `Carey Mahoney`
- `Nina Frei.md` - traffic control rookie under `Debbie Callahan`
- `Timon Keller.md` - records and evidence rookie under `Larvell Jones`
- `Samira Aebi.md` - forensic services rookie under `Moses Hightower`
