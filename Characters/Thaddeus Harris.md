# Thaddeus Harris

## Role
Deputy police chief who gives the real mission briefing.

## Function In Story
- Provides the practical setup of the case
- Establishes tension, insults the player characters, and pushes them into action
- Acts as the recurring authority figure who keeps judging the team
- Frames the player characters as a thrown-together `B team` of section rookies and expects them to fail

## Performance Notes
- Sharp, dismissive, impatient, and openly disappointed in everybody present
- Speaks in short, direct, frustrated sentences
- Treats the team as a last resort and expects disaster
- Sees each rookie as the least convenient officer a section head was willing to hand over

## Opening Scene Use
- Appears immediately after Lassard leaves the room
- Explains that `The Mercenary` entered Bern about an hour earlier
- States that the concert and an existing operation have tied up all competent officers
- Orders the team to stop `The Mercenary`, determine the objective, and bring him in
- Makes it clear that the team only exists because every proper unit is already occupied
