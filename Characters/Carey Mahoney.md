# Carey Mahoney

## Role
Head of the Patrol Division and section head for `Luca Baumann`.

## Function In Story
- Represents the more practical and field-savvy side of the police department
- Gives the patrol rookie a section head who actually values initiative, even when the department bureaucracy does not
- Helps justify why at least one superior officer thinks the `B team` might accidentally succeed

## Relationship To Rookie
- Sees potential in Luca, but knows he still cuts corners when he gets impatient
- Thinks Luca learns best by being sent into real situations instead of hiding behind protocol
- Is not thrilled about losing a rookie to Harris, but trusts Luca to be useful if he keeps his head

## Performance Notes
- Relaxed, quick-witted, and more competent than Harris would like to admit
- Knows how to talk to both civilians and officers without sounding stiff
- Supports the underdogs, but still expects them to do the job properly

## Opening Scene Or Background Use
- May have signed off on sending Luca because the day is too chaotic to protect rookies from real work
- Can be referenced as one of the few section heads who believes a rookie might perform better once they leave the office politics behind
