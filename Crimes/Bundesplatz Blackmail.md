# Bundesplatz Blackmail

## Type
Political and money-driven extortion.

## Core Plan
`The Mercenary` plants hidden explosives and a public broadcast threat near `Bundesplatz` and demands a payout in exchange for preventing mass panic during New Year's Eve operations.

## Bern Old Town Tie
- `Bundesplatz`
- `Federal Palace`
- surrounding federal and public event spaces

## Why It Works
- Combines political embarrassment with financial pressure
- Creates crowd panic risk in the heart of Bern
- Lets the police team stumble through evacuation attempts and bomb scares

## First Clue
A scrambled threat message references a timed action at `Bundesplatz`, but the wording is so theatrical that Harris first assumes it is a prank.

## Possible Finale
A chaotic confrontation during a partial evacuation at `Bundesplatz`, with the team trying to identify the real device among fake ones.
