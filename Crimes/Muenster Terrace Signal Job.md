# Muenster Terrace Signal Job

## Type
Political sabotage.

## Core Plan
`The Mercenary` uses the `Bern Minster` terrace and surrounding elevation as a signal or trigger point for a remote device aimed at a high-profile event in the city.

## Bern Old Town Tie
- `Bern Minster`
- `Muensterplattform`
- `Muenstergasse`

## Why It Works
- Uses a dramatic and elevated Bern location
- Supports surveillance, hidden equipment, and a layered clue trail
- Creates a strong visual finale with vertical movement and city views

## First Clue
An unusual technical case and antenna fragments are found near the terrace after a suspicious overnight access report.

## Possible Finale
A race across the terrace and surrounding stair routes as the team tries to stop a remote trigger before a public ceremony begins.

## Act Structure

### Act 1
`The Mercenary` surveys sightlines, terrace access, and elevated routes around `Bern Minster`.

### Act 2
He gathers technical equipment, concealment gear, and forged access material that could fit multiple sabotage or infiltration plans.

### Act 3
He tests or positions the signal setup on the terrace, revealing the `Muensterplattform` as the final operational site.

### Act 4
The group faces him during the live signal or trigger operation at the Minster terrace.
