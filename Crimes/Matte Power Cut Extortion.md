# Matte Power Cut Extortion

## Type
Sabotage and money-driven extortion.

## Core Plan
`The Mercenary` tampers with infrastructure connected to the `Mattequartier` and threatens rolling failures across parts of the Old Town unless a payoff is delivered.

## Bern Old Town Tie
- `Mattequartier`
- riverside utility routes
- older service and delivery infrastructure linked to the lower city

## Why It Works
- Gives Bern a gritty underlevel beneath the postcard city image
- Supports technical clues and escalating civic disruption
- Lets the players investigate both public disorder and hidden sabotage

## First Clue
Repeated failures in the same district show signs of deliberate tampering rather than overload from New Year's Eve demand.

## Possible Finale
A confrontation near the lower river district while lights flicker, alarms fail, and the team loses radio contact at the worst possible moment.

## Act Structure

### Act 1
`The Mercenary` studies infrastructure routes, lower-city access, and service connections in the `Mattequartier`.

### Act 2
He acquires sabotage tools, communications gear, and forged access items that still fit other crime theories.

### Act 3
He triggers or prepares a controlled failure in the lower district, revealing that the extortion plan depends on the `Mattequartier` network.

### Act 4
The group confronts him during the staged infrastructure disruption in the riverside district.
