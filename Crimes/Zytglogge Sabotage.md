# Zytglogge Sabotage

## Type
Public sabotage.

## Core Plan
`The Mercenary` rigs the `Zytglogge` mechanism to fail in a spectacular way during a key public moment, using the confusion in the Old Town streets as cover for escape or a second operation.

## Bern Old Town Tie
- `Zytglogge`
- `Kramgasse`
- tourist-heavy arcade routes nearby

## Why It Works
- Uses one of Bern's most iconic landmarks
- Gives the session a memorable ticking-clock structure
- Fits both physical comedy and genuine public danger

## First Clue
A maintenance worker reports forged service credentials and missing access records connected to the clock tower.

## Possible Finale
A vertical showdown in or around the `Zytglogge`, with bells, gears, crowds, and terrible police coordination all going wrong at once.
