# Swiss National Bank Robbery

## Type
Money-driven heist with diversion and sabotage elements.

## Core Plan
`The Mercenary` targets the `Swiss National Bank` building near `Bundesplatz`, using forged police instructions, false emergency procedures, and New Year's Eve chaos to gain controlled access to money or secured valuables.

## Bern Old Town Tie
- `Swiss National Bank` building in the federal square area
- `Bundesplatz`
- nearby Old Town escape and diversion routes

## Why It Works
- Strong central heist objective with immediate stakes
- Easy to disguise as a political emergency at first
- Perfect for a fake evacuation, bad police coordination, and last-minute realization of the real target

## First Clue
A security officer reports receiving valid-looking emergency instructions that reroute normal SNB access procedures through police channels.

## Possible Finale
A collapsing fake-security operation around the bank, with the team realizing too late that they helped seal the wrong perimeter.

## Act Structure

### Act 1
`The Mercenary` studies emergency response patterns, perimeter routines, and access behavior around the federal square and bank area.

### Act 2
He acquires forged police orders, control gear, and access tools that could still support blackmail, document theft, or a transfer hijack.

### Act 3
He prepares a false emergency perimeter and final access window, revealing the `Swiss National Bank` as the true target.

### Act 4
The group faces him during the robbery attempt at the `Swiss National Bank` building.
