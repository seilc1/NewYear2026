# Case Premise

## Core Premise
On the morning of 31.12.2026, the player characters are assembled as an unwanted backup unit because Bern's competent police resources are tied up by a major concert and an ongoing operation.

At the same time, the known criminal `The Mercenary` has entered the city. The team must discover his objective, stop the plan before the city celebrations peak, and arrest him.

## Core Tensions
- The clock is running toward New Year's Eve festivities
- The police department does not trust the player characters
- The villain's motive and target are unknown at the start
- Public chaos can escalate quickly in crowded Bern locations

## Tone Target
- Crime-solving first
- `Police Academy` parody throughout
- Escalating incompetence, misunderstandings, and comic set pieces without losing the main investigation
